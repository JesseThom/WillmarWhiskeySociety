History

Our Story

The Willmar Whisky Society, established in 2019 and recognized as a 501(c)(3) organization, is a community of whisky enthusiasts. Centered around a shared passion for whisky, our primary focus is fostering a sense of camaraderie and building a community based on this common interest. Additionally, we are committed to making a positive impact in our community through philanthropic initiatives.

We warmly invite you to join us at one of our upcoming monthly samplings, where you can share your love for whisky with fellow enthusiasts and be part of our mission to make a meaningful difference in our community.
